About

Strategy meets
execution

I'm a product leader and venture builder with over a decade of experience taking ideas from napkin sketch to product-market fit. My background spans BCG Digital Ventures, Rocket Internet, and a range of early-stage to growth-stage companies — always at the intersection of product strategy, technology, and real user impact.

I work best in the messy early phases: shaping product vision, running discovery, building lean MVPs, and setting up the teams and systems to scale. Increasingly, I focus on AI-enabled products and technology that supports wellbeing, human development, and conscious living.

I bring strategic clarity without losing the craft. I ship fast, think in systems, and care deeply about building things that are not just useful — but meaningful.
